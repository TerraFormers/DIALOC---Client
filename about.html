For any point on Earth, there is one on exactly the other side - its antipode. A novel phenomenon, albeit one packed with the existential significance of anything singularly rare.

DIALOC is a graphical interface for exploring these isolated relationships, using the best of modern public-resource mapping tools to give users a binary glimpse of places otherwise as separate as two can be.
